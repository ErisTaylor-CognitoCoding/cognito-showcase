## The Prompt I Gave

Not a spec. Not a requirements doc. Just this:

```
I want to create an app for dog walking businesses. It needs a diary where they
can schedule walks on. Put dogs into groups for walks with their photos. Store
owner information, dog information, emergency details, and allow emergency vet
treatment forms to be uploaded. Other things could involve a route planner for
picking up dogs and mileage recording. Invoicing could be another thing. And
somewhere to note cancellations.
```

Plain English, no jargon. Replit planned it out — six core areas — and started building.

---

## What the AI Built

WagTracker — an app for dog walking businesses, built from a conversation on a beach walk with my dog walker. It schedules walks, manages dog and owner profiles (entered once, linked everywhere), handles vet consent forms, plans pickup routes, tracks mileage, generates invoices, and takes Stripe payments. Built as a PWA so it installs straight from Chrome — no App Store needed. £9.99/month, 7-day free trial at [wagtracker.app](https://wagtracker.app).

The golden rule baked into every screen: **enter your information once**. One dog profile, one owner record, one vet entry. Everything links back. The app does the chasing.
