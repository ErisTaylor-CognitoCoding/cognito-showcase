## Overview

SoloTutorLite brings lesson planning, interactive whiteboard, one-click video, AI-generated reports, and invoicing into a single app. No juggling five different tools. No admin eating into the evening.

Built by a tutor who still teaches every day.

**Who it's for:** Solo tutors who need whiteboard and video in one place, want professional invoices without the faff, and are tired of using 5 apps to do one job.

## Features

- **Lesson Planning** — Drag-and-drop weekly planner. Assign subjects, time slots, and notes per student.
- **Interactive Whiteboard** — Built-in whiteboard with scientific calculator, multiple backgrounds, document upload, and screen sharing. Replaces Lessonspace / Bitpaper.
- **Video Link** — One-click in-app video sessions with students. No Zoom subscription needed.
- **Student Management** — Contact details, subjects, lesson history, and parent info per student.
- **AI Lesson Plans & Reports** — Generate lesson plans and end-of-session reports with one click. Copy-ready for the tutor to send through their own channel.
- **Invoicing** — Professional PDF invoices for parents. Monthly or per-lesson billing.
- **Simple Accounts** — Track income and expenses with an end-of-month summary for tax returns.
