## Overview

WagTracker replaces the notebook, the spreadsheet, the WhatsApp admin, and the invoice template with one purpose-built app. Schedule walks, manage clients and their dogs, track cancellations, handle invoicing, and keep emergency vet details on file — all from a phone.

Built because a real dog walker asked for it.

**Who it's for:** Solo dog walkers managing 10–50+ dogs who juggle bookings via WhatsApp and notebooks and want to look professional without hiring an accountant.

## Features

- **Walk Scheduling** — Drag-and-drop daily walks. Group walks, solo walks, and recurring bookings.
- **Dog & Owner Profiles** — Breed, temperament, allergies, medications, vet info, and emergency contacts per dog.
- **Walk Groups** — Organise dogs into compatible groups; track which dogs walk well together.
- **Cancellation Tracking** — Log cancellations with reasons, spot patterns, and protect your income.
- **Invoicing** — Generate professional invoices weekly or monthly.
- **Route Planning** — Save favourite walking routes with distance and duration metadata for quick re-use.
- **Emergency Vet Forms** — Instant access to every dog's vet details and emergency contacts.
