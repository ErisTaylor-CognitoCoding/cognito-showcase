## What We're Building

Pac-Man — the iconic dot-eating maze game. A grid-locked player, four ghosts with distinct personalities, power pellets, frightened mode, and a score that climbs with each dot eaten.

The coding challenge: how do four ghosts chase you without being able to see through walls? How does Blinky chase directly while Pinky ambushes ahead of you? Ghost AI turns out to be simpler than it looks — and understanding it changes how you read every enemy in every game.
