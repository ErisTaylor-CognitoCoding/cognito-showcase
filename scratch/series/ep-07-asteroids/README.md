## What We're Building

Asteroids — rotation, thrust, screen-wrapping, and rocks that split into smaller rocks when you shoot them.

The coding challenge: in Pong and Frogger, sprites move in straight lines at fixed speeds. In Asteroids, the ship points in any direction and momentum carries it forward even after you stop thrusting. That's vector movement — velocity as two numbers (x-speed and y-speed) that combine to produce direction. It's how physics engines work, and once you understand it, you see it everywhere.
